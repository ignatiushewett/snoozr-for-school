About Snoozr

Snoozr is a prototype sleep tracking application designed for HSC Software Engineering Task #2. It targets high school and university students who struggle with poor sleeping habits, offering a progressive, anxiety-free alternative to apps like Sleep Cycle or Pillow that can cause Orthosomnia.
